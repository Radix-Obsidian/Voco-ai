# Voco Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 23, 2026
**Last Updated:** February 23, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Voice Audio
Voco captures microphone audio locally on your device and streams it to our speech-to-text provider (Deepgram) for transcription. Raw audio is never stored on our servers. Transcripts are retained for up to 30 days for quality improvement, then permanently deleted.

### 1.2 Usage Metrics
We collect anonymized usage data including: session duration, feature usage frequency, error rates, and platform information. This data contains no personally identifiable information (PII).

### 1.3 Authentication Data
When you create an account, we store your email address, hashed password, and subscription status via Supabase Auth.

### 1.4 Payment Data
Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We never store credit card numbers, CVVs, or full card details. We retain only a Stripe customer ID for subscription management.

## 2. Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Deepgram | Speech-to-text | Audio stream (not stored) |
| Cartesia | Text-to-speech | Text prompts |
| Supabase | Auth & database | Email, user ID |
| Stripe | Billing | Email, payment info |
| CrabNebula | App distribution | Platform, version |

## 3. Data Retention
- **Voice transcripts**: 30 days, then deleted
- **Usage metrics**: 12 months, anonymized
- **Account data**: Until account deletion
- **Payment records**: As required by law (typically 7 years)

## 4. Your Code Never Leaves Your Machine
Voco's MCP Gateway executes all file operations, terminal commands, and code searches locally on your device. Your source code is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

## 5. Your Rights

### GDPR (EU/EEA Users)
You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and port your data. Contact privacy@voco.ai.

### CCPA (California Users)
You have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of data sales. We do not sell personal information.

## 6. Data Security
We use encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel with multi-factor authentication.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Voco is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We will notify users of material changes via email and in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect.
